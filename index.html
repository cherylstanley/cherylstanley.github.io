Projects

AWARE - Apple Watch Alcohol Risk Estimator (2023-2024)

AWARE is a dual platform app, for iPhone and paired Apple Watch users, which aims to mitigate alcohol overdose and overconsumption risks through real-time user analysis and incorporation of several safety features based on a user's predicted intoxication level. It incorporates a Machine Learning model to make real-time predictions on intoxication level based on a user's biometric data including walking steadiness and heart rate, which has never been done before in any existing solution.

The app then takes several automatic actions based on the level predicted including notifying emergency services in case of a Level 3 (Severe) determination, as well as notifying / texting location to emergency contacts and enabling ride share services (using Uber API) to the user's current location in case of a Level 2 (Drunk) determination. The CS Capstone Project culminated in our work being presented in front of a panel of judges at the annual CS Summit (2024) where our team placed 2nd overall and received a cash prize of $1500.
